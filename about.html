About EMCA MOTORS LIMITED

Established in 2018 in the heart of Lodwar, EMCA MOTORS LIMITED has grown to become the most trusted garage and spare parts supplier in Turkana County.

We specialize in supplying genuine automotive parts and delivering professional mechanical services to private car owners, matatu operators, NGOs, and government fleets.
