About EMCA MOTORS LIMITED

Established in 2022 in the heart of Lodwar, EMCA MOTORS LIMITED has grown to become the most trusted garage and spare parts supplier in Turkana County.

We specialize in supplying genuine automotive parts and delivering professional mechanical services to private car owners, matatu operators, NGOs, and government fleets.

Our Mission

"Keep every vehicle on Kenyan roads running safely and affordably."

20+ Years Combined Experience

Our team of 12 certified technicians have trained in Nairobi, Eldoret and Mombasa workshops.

4,800+ Vehicles Serviced

From Toyota Hilux to heavy trucks – we’ve kept them all on the road.
