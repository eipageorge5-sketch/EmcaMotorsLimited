Frequently Asked Questions

Do you stock genuine Toyota parts?

Yes! All our parts are sourced directly from authorized distributors in Kenya.

How fast is your service?

Most oil changes and alignments are completed within 90 minutes.

Do I need an appointment for alignment or balancing?

While we accept walk-ins for parts purchases, we recommend booking an appointment for alignment, balancing, or mechanical repairs to ensure minimal wait time.

What is the difference between wheel alignment and balancing?

Alignment adjusts the angles of the wheels to manufacturer specs, while balancing corrects weight distribution around the wheel to prevent steering wheel vibrations.

Do you deliver outside Lodwar?

Currently, our delivery services are exclusively available within Lodwar town. This allows us to ensure the fastest delivery times and maintain the quality of our service for our local customers.

Is cash on delivery available?

Absolutely. You can also pay via M-Pesa or bank transfer.
